How to convert, compress and edit video

EaseUS VideoKit makes your video editing as easy as a few clicks away. Let's show you how to make it.

  •   Convert video
  •   Compress video
  •   Edit video
Step 1
Launch EaseUS VideoKit and choose the "Video Converter". Add or drag your video or audio files to the program.
Step 2
Set up the target file format, specify the device that the video will be compatible with, choose to adjust for sharing on YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, or other social platforms, and confirm the resolution up to 8K properly.
Step 3
Locate a file folder or destination hard drive to save the output video files. Then click to start the conversion. Once finished, open the video to preview it in the built-in media player.
Step 1
Download and launch EaseUS VideoKit on your computer. Select "Video Compressor" from the left side.
Step 2
Click "Choose Files" to select the video files you wish to compress. Customize your video size, targeted output format, bitrate, resolution, and more. Click "Compress" button.
Step 3
Once the process is completed, you can go to the "Finished" tab to check the compressed videos.
Step 1
Launch EaseUS VideoKit and choose the "Video Editor". Select an editing feature you want to have a try. Here choose to trim a video as an example.
Step 2
Click the Add Your File button and select the video. Drag the video track, drop the marker where you want to cut the video, or select a clip of the video.
Step 3
Click the "Export" button to output the trimmed video. Choose the most suitable option for formats or resolutions. Also, you're able to merge multiple video clips into one if there are.
